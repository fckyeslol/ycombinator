The insight

Most AI tools stop at generation. Prewave runs the whole loop — from scraping what works to attributing what grew.

A full content team, run by one operator.

  1. 01

    Scrape & score

    Monitors top creators across 6 platforms and surfaces the outliers — posts beating their own author's average.

  2. 02

    Brief & script

    AI turns each winner into a brief and a script written in the account's own voice.

  3. 03

    Swipe & QA

    The team approves or rejects in a Tinder-style board, behind a quality gate.

  4. 04

    Produce

    An editor kanban takes raw footage to final cut, with role-based access for the team.

  5. 05

    Verify & publish

    Fact-checks against live web search, then schedules across Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn and Substack.

  6. 06

    Attribute

    Tracks which content actually drove follower growth — and feeds it back.

↺ Attribution feeds the next scrape — the loop compounds

Why it works

Where generators stop, Prewave keeps going.
